About Us

"Where Tradition Meets Flavor". Located in the heart of San Francisco's Mission District, Paprika is a family-owned restaurant that has been delighting diners for over 10 years. Our passion is traditional Central European food, and we pride ourselves on offering an authentic dining experience.
